NEW ZEALAND

INNOVATIVE LEARNING, BREATHTAKING CAMPUSES, AND A SUPPORTIVE ENVIRONMENT FOR INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS

NEW ZEALAND OFFERS A WORLD-CLASS EDUCATION SYSTEM WITH A FOCUS ON RESEARCH, CREATIVITY, AND PRACTICAL SKILLS. ITS UNIVERSITIES AND INSTITUTES ARE GLOBALLY RECOGNIZED, PROVIDING DEGREES THAT OPEN DOORS TO CAREERS WORLDWIDE.

WHY STUDY IN NEW ZEALAND

STUDY IN NEW ZEALAND FOR WORLD-CLASS EDUCATION, HANDS-ON LEARNING, AND STRONG CAREER OPPORTUNITIES. ENJOY A SAFE, WELCOMING ENVIRONMENT AND BREATHTAKING CAMPUSES WHILE GAINING SKILLS RECOGNIZED GLOBALLY.

BENEFITS OF STUDYING IN NEW ZEALAND

  • ✔ Opportunity To Study In The High QS World Ranking Universities And Technological Colleges
  • ✔ Researched-Based Education
  • ✔ The Classroom Ratio Of Tutor-Student Is Very Small, With A High Level Of Tutorstudent Interaction
  • ✔ NZ Extends A Warm And Friendly Welcome To All International Students

FAMILY SPONSOR

New Zealand Allows Citizens And Residents To Sponsor Eligible Family Members To Join Them. This Includes Partners, Dependent Children, And Sometimes Parents Or Grandparents. Sponsored Family Members Can Live, Study, And Work In New Zealand, Making It Easier For Families To Stay Together While Enjoying The Country’s Opportunities.

POST-STUDY WORK RIGHTS

  • ✔ The Post Graduation Work Permit (PGWP) Allows International Students To Work In New Zealand By Granting The Work Permit For Up To Three Years.
  • ✔ The Minimum Study Period For Applying Work Permit Is One Year
  • ✔ Post Graduate Diploma, Masters Degree, And Ph.D. Graduates Are Eligible For A 3-Years Post-Study Work Permit.
